Why Taskly is Different

We built Taskly on three core principles to provide a tool that respects you and your data.

Absolute Privacy

All processing happens on your computer. Your files are never uploaded to a server, ensuring your data remains 100% private and secure.

Blazing Speed

By leveraging your browser's power with WebAssembly, tasks like video conversion and image editing are completed in seconds, not minutes.

Completely Free

Taskly is a passion project. It's free to use, with no ads, no sign-ups, and no premium tiers. Just powerful tools, available to everyone.
